POST 04

DISCLAIMER: poorly written. this post is very informal.

Like my previous idea (Manila’s traffic congestion), my intent is to help the people who I know in the most minuscule possible way, but over time, I want it to affect them strongly to a point that they kind of depend on in their daily life. The reason why I am abandoning my original idea is because I want to tackle on a challenge that I have to deal with in my everyday life. Manila’s traffic congestion does not really affect my life other than the times I visit the city. My new direction is exploring how to manage our family members from within in a micro and macro level.

How this idea came to be is simple, I was at home and my siblings and I were fighting over who’s turn it was to take out the garbage. It got me thinking, if there was a centralised system where we can record and schedule or family life, things would be much easier. It’s not all about managing chores either. My family usually chips in for presents, it would be a nice idea if theres a way where we can all put in money in one single app. I agree, its much easier to e-transfer, but I think using the e-transfer isn’t the end of it all. I believer theres a way to make it much easier. (this type of system can also work for a group of friends)

I try to stay active and I try to eat healthy. I try to influence my family members as much as possible as well. And they do, they truly try but I think a better way to manage our eating habits could be managed a better way. Recently, I checked out family fridge and our pantry and I noticed a lot of food turning into waste because of the abundance of food we own. (it would be a cool idea to have a voting system what we are having for dinner on a Friday night)

Vacation is a big part of my family. We try to fly out once a year (I fly twice a year) and it would be awesome if we can all pitch in to a family vacation in a more organised manner. This idea branched of and I thought of a more bigger solution. What about a financial feature where we pay off all our bills within the family? like for example, car insurance, phone bill, or etc. what about a feature like a swear jar? family members put in a small amount into the saving jar.

I know this is bad but, I see the potential in this project to be actually placed into real life. this system would gather data and sell them to big companies. I mean personally, I see no harm to it. Why would I keep it a secret what my family eats every week?
